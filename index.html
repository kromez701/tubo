      How does it support the immune system?

      Chlorine, heavy metals, and other impurities in tap water can irritate your child’s fragile skin barrier—making their skin more vulnerable and uncomfortable. When their skin is constantly under stress, their body may have to work harder to stay in balance. By filtering out these irritants, you’re helping support your child’s natural skin defences and overall comfort.

      check_box

      Does it work for adults too?

      Yes, even though our filter is mainly marketed for children’s sensitive skin, it’s just as effective for adults. The filter removes impurities, chlorine, and harsh chemicals from bathwater, making it beneficial for anyone looking to reduce skin irritation, enjoy softer skin, smoother hair or simply have a cleaner bathing experience.

      check_box

      Does it require maintenance?

      No, Tubo doesn’t need any maintenance until it filters 2,500 gallons of water. After that, simply replace the filter.

      check_box

      How long does shipping take?

      We process and ship all orders within 24 hours. Standard shipping typically takes 5-12 business days, depending on your location. We also offer expedited shipping options at checkout for faster delivery.

      check_box

      How effective is Tubo at removing specific contaminants?

      Tubo's advanced 8-stage filtration system is designed to remove a wide range of contaminants, including:

      • Chlorine: Removes up to 99% of chlorine, which can cause skin irritation and dryness.
      • Heavy Metals: Filters out harmful heavy metals like lead, mercury, and arsenic.
      • Bacteria: Removes up to 99.9% of bacteria and viruses for a hygienic bathtime.
      check_box

      How long does the filter last?

      Each Tubo filter lasts for approximately 2,500 gallons of water, which is about 6-8 months of regular use for most families. However, you may need to replace your filter sooner if you notice a decrease in water flow or if the water starts to have an unusual odor. We recommend marking your calendar and replacing your filter every 6 months to ensure optimal performance.

      check_box

      Can Tubo help with specific skin conditions like eczema?

      While Tubo is not a medical treatment, many parents have reported improvements in their baby's skin conditions after using Tubo. The gentle, purified water can help soothe irritated skin and reduce the risk of further irritation from harsh contaminants. If your baby has a specific skin condition, we always recommend consulting with your pediatrician or dermatologist for personalized advice.

      check_box

      How do I install Tubo?

      Installing Tubo is a breeze! Simply remove your faucet's aerator (if applicable) and attach Tubo securely. No tools are required, and the process takes just seconds. An adapter for various faucet types are included.

      check_box

      Is Tubo safe for babies?

      Absolutely! Tubo is specifically designed with your baby's delicate skin in mind. Our advanced filtration system removes harsh contaminants, leaving only pure, gentle water that's perfect for sensitive skin.

      check_box

      What if I'm not satisfied with Tubo?

      We're confident that you'll love Tubo, but if for any reason you're not completely satisfied, we offer a 60-Day Money-Back Guarantee. Simply contact our customer support team within 60 days of your purchase, and we'll issue a full refund.

      Does it affect the flow rate?

      The filter is designed to thoroughly purify the water passing through it. For faucets with an extremely high flow rate, this may result in a slight delay in the water flow. This slower flow rate is necessary to effectively remove chemicals and contaminants. To prevent any overflow issues, we have included a plastic cone that can be placed on top of the filter. If this does not resolve the issue, we recommend adjusting the water flow to a slower rate.

      check_box
